The First Plague

A new twist on the Zombie Apocalypse genre! Logan and Luke Matthews are brothers who are on opposite sides of the city when a zombie apocalypse kicks off. With friends and families at their side, they journey to an old church of a mysterious, ancient order. When an unstoppable creature appears, they realize there is more at stake than they could ever have imagined. Tracking their every move is a pseudo-military faction with an arcane-wielding madman at its helm. At the center of it all is the brothers' ancient bloodline. This is a zombie story of BIBLICAL proportions.

Novice science fiction writer Kevin Port is employed as a full time Police Officer for a major metropolitan city. He lives in Pennsylvania with his amazing wife and four fur babies. As his first foray into the writing world, this is the first in a line of supernatural stories he wishes to share with the world. He brings his unique flare and an abundance of perfectly delivered jokes- “perfect” being a subjective term- to the frontline of a dark and expansive take on the zombie apocalypse genre. Kevin draws his influences from all forms of horror media. Including but not limited to movies, video games, books, and graphic novels. He is a strong advocate of the belief that any form of creation is art, especially video games.
